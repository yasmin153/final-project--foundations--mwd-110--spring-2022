About Fatimah

Fatimah is a Kuwaiti graphic designer and a painter. Originally, she was goint to be majoring in genetics but decided to switch a couple weeks before she started unuversity. She first picked up a painting brush when she took a painting class at 19 years old. Her painting mostly is tribute to her family. It was a way for her to express her love for her parents. However, her graphic design work shows her passion for fitness, typography, and branding. She hope to change the concept of art in Kuwait and expand the knowledge of it.
